Overview

Do you need a pointing device for use in an MRI environment? Our joystick combines a short handle that can be operated with one or two fingers with a solid, stable base. One handle mounted button and four body mounted buttons allow subjects to provide additional inputs.

Features

  • Analog magnet room circuit design allows interference-free operation in the MR environment
  • 10-bit signal digitization offers superior control for demanding experiments
  • Two axes and 5 buttons (1 shaft mounted, 4 on the body) provide tactile feedback for positive subject responses (Tactile buttons depress at 3 Newtons of force or approx. 300g)
  • USB HID interface appears as a standard USB HID joystick when plugged into PC, Mac, or Linux systems. No driver intstallation required
  • Durable plastic unibody construction
  • 300 series stainless steel joystick gimbal provides long life while also being non-ferrous
  • Return-to-center spring (if your application does not allow a return to center spring, this can be removed; please specify when ordering)
  • Optional analog output (0-5v) for faster sampling via DAC available
  • Dimensions: 8 inches length, 5 inches width, 1.5 inches height (shaft extends 3 inches above body)
