Pointer for MRI

Overview

Do you need a hand held pointing device for use in an MRI environment without extra buttons? This hand held, thumb operated pointing device has allows subjects to click using the thumb stick.

Features

  • Analog magnet room circuit design allows interference-free operation in the MR environment
  • 10-bit signal digitization offers superior control for demanding experiments
  • Two axes and 1 buttons (thumb stick can be clicked)
  • USB HID interface appears as a standard USB HID joystick when plugged into PC, Mac, or Linux systems. No driver intstallation required
  • ABS plastic construction
  • Thumb controlled and can be easily held by the subject without a fixture
  • Return-to-center
  • Optional analog outputs (0-5v) available
  • Dimensions: 4 inches length, 1.5 inches width, 1.5 inches height
  • For research use only
