Overview

A touch pad available in iPad sized 10", 19", and 6" sizes that can be used with either fingers or a stylus as a pointing device. The touch pad provides a great way for subjects to provide input in a familiar form factor. Use it like the touch pad on a laptop as a replacement for a mouse or with the included stylus to capture handwriting or drawing. The included drivers provide many options for customization.

Features

  • 12-bit signal digitization offers superior control for demanding experiments
  • 10ms response time
  • Available in multiple sizes
  • Can be used with a finger or a stylus (provided)
  • Durable plastic construction
  • Shipping weight: 5 pounds
  • Dimensions: 10 inches length, 8 inches width (10 inch model, refer to brochure for other sizes)
  • Note: this device is not multi-touch capable (e.g. two finger scrolling or two finger gestures)
  • Optional controller available with higher spatial and temporal resolution and 0-5v BNC outputs
  • For research use only
