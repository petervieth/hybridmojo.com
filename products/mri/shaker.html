Pneumatic Shaker for MRI

Request Quote

Overview

Investigating the effects of touch stimuli of different frequencies on the brain? This shaker can produce vibrations of roughly 60hz to 120hz. The shaker itself can be placed against the subjects skin and held in place with elastic or rubber bands.

Features

  • Adjustable frequency of vibration with coarse and fine adjustments
  • Adjustable vibration amplitude
  • Triggered by +5v signal from a DAC
  • Made of plastic and brass, powered by compressed air (air compressor not included)
  • Durable Delrin plastic construction
  • Shipping weight: 25 pounds
  • Dimensions: 10 inches length, 8 inches width
  • For research use only
