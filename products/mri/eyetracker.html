Overview

An NTSC eyetracker camera for fMRI research. This camera can be mounted to a head coil or other device to capture video of eye movements for eye tracking.

Features

  • Up to 525 vertical scan lines resolution (NTSC) with 30Hz refresh rate (depends on video capture hardware)
  • Standard RCA output
  • Dual IR LEDs with user adjustable illumination level for even, fine tuned iris illumination
  • Adjustable mounting
  • Small size
  • Shipping weight: 5 pounds
  • For research use only
