Overview

A MIDI keyboard with 33 or 37 full size keys. The perfect solution for your research into music and the brain!

Features

  • 33 (or 37) full size keys are easy for subjects to use with minimal adjustment from a real piano
  • Designed to fit in most MRI bores (60cm diameter)
  • Standard USB MIDI interface – works with all modern operating systems (Windows, Mac, Linux) and sequencing programs (Cakewalk, etc)
  • Rubber springs for the best feel without metal springs
  • Shipping weight: 15 pounds
